Feature: My Account
    Scenario Outline: User can access his own account page and register as a new user
        Given that I open Chrome Version 136.0.7103.93 (Official Build) (64-bit) and navigate to https://ecommerce-playground.lambdatest.io/index.php?route=common/home
        And the home page is displayed as expected
        And I click on "My account" link
        Then the My Account page is displayed as expected
        And When I click on "Continue"
        And I fill-in all required fields
        And I check the "Subscribe" radio button
        And I tick "I have read and agree to the Privacy Policy"
        And I click on "Continue"
        Then my account is registered as expected
        And When I click on "Continue"
        Then I access "My Account" page
